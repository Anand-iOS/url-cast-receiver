Details

There are two methods for casting, with pros and cons:

  1. Load an iframe (You will have trouble loading a url with x-frame-options: same origin.)
  2. Change the window location (You will lose control when you load the url over the reciever.)
Also, notice the chromecast viewport is 1280x720 and the default background is black (noticable on location style loads).
